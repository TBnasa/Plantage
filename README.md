"A digital forest growing with your memories."

Plantage is a privacy-focused journal application that allows you to grow a personal digital tree through your daily notes, photos, and videos. 

## Features ‚ú®
* **Organic Growth:** A new branch develops every 24 hours based on your activity.
* **Memory Tracking:** Attach photos, videos, and text to specific days/branches.
* **Multilingual:** Supports English, Turkish, Russian, Chinese, German, and French.
* **Privacy First:** Your data stays on your device.
* **Open Source:** Licensed under GPLv3.
